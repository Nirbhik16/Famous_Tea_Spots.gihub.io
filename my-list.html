Chai Pee lo Friends: 5 famous Chai Spots in Chandigarh

Nirbhik Kakkar
Last updated Feb 27, 2022

  • MBA Chai Wala
    Booth no 12, inner Market, 35C, Sector 35, Chandigarh, 160022

    “MBA, MBA, MBA was all that was going in and out, day and night in my mind” was the first statement that came out of Prafull Billore's mind when asked about his tryst with destiny.
    Prafull Billore is popularly known as the MBA Chaiwala. He is the proud owner of the 'MBA Chaiwala' franchise, which is now a three-crore empire built on 'chai' and his dreams.Praful Billore’s success journey did not end with his chai shop. His success story reached far and wide, and guess what? He was invited by IIM-Ahmedabad, the B-school he was yearning to pursue his MBA to share his business journey with the post-graduate students. He also got invites from various other IIM’s and Harvard Business School to address his journey.
    Today MBA Chaiwala has over 20 employees and a business turnover of three crores this fiscal year.

    Recommendation: MBA Special Chai + an order of Ahmedabad Bun.

  • Chai Sutta Bar
    Booth No. 167, Mcm College Road, Chandigarh Sector 36, Chandigarh - 160036

    Chai Sutta Bar which is Famous for Serving Hygienic Herbal Products with hygienic since 2017 now opened at Chandigarh . Currently operating at successfully 25 locations across the country serves freshly brewed tea,coffee and a lot more while bringing back fresh nostalgia of good old Days. The fact is that the guests are served herbal products that can enjoy the taste along it being healthy at the same time.
    Ambience is no less than bar but products are served at very nominal price with highest quality of taste. The speciality of Chai Sutta Bar is strong adrak ki chai ,hot masala chai. Evergreen kulhad tea has undergone through flavourful reinvention where combinations like paan chai, rose chai, chocolate chai and irani chai will emerged as winners for the young residents in Chandigarh . Some new flavours are to t added in it making this rainy season a hit in the city.

    Recommendation: CSB Special Cold Coffee + Paneer Taka Tak Sandwich.

  • Desi Urban Chai
    Booth no. 25, Sector 20D, Sector 20, Chandigarh, 160020

    Desi Urban Chai is a premium tea & coffee brand started in 2020. They are dedicated to mordanize the tea culture by serving premium quality chai and coffee yet maintaining the affordablity of their products.
    Authentic flavors has been served rightly. That is why kulhad's has been chosen as medium to serve the chai. Kulhad's enhances severability and also leaves it own aroma and flavors thereby enhancing the overall experience.
    Along with aromatic chai, they also focus on serving light bites and snacks. Their aim is to blend the QSR model along with our delicious chai. This includes variety of burgers, sandwiches, maggi, fries, garlic breads, cheese toasts and patties.

    Recommendation: Ginger Tea + Corn Mayo Sandwiches.

  • Chai Wali Gali
    Booth No 8 Mini Market Sector 8, 8B, Sector 8, Chandigarh, 160009

    Go ahead and Get talli on Chai at Chai Wali Gali in Elante Mall Chandigarh. This quirky little outletcan be found among other food outlets located at the Food court of Elante Mall. One can enjoy the bird's eye view of the courtyard at Elante during the rains while sipping on to a hot cup of tea and gorging on scrunchy snacks from this tea spot in Chandigarh. Pair their flavored or classic tea with a plate of pakoras and your monsoons are sorted!

    Recommendation: Cold Coffee + Mix Pakora

  • Chaayos
    178, MW Area, Industrial Area Phase I, Chandigarh, 160002

    One of the most-loved tea spots in Chandigarh, Chaayos is a rage among the chai lovers in the city.
    Serving over 50 types of teas to Chai Addicts in City beautiful, Chaayos in Elante Mall Chandigarh is a beautifully swanked up cafe cum tea lounge. From desi cutting to kulhad wali chai to Kashmiri kava to saffron tea, one can sip on steaming hot chai along with scrumptious snacks like bun maska, keema pav, pakora platter, pizza paratha, and more. A fantastic getaway overlooking the courtyard in Elante Mall, Chaayos in Chandigarh offers a respite during the monsoon along with a cup of chai and a plate of mouth-watering food. Go and Enjoy the monsoons at this tea spot in Chandigarh!

    Recommendation: Desi Chai + Baarish Wale Pakore
